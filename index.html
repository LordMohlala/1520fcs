About Us

We supply and fit a variety of aluminium, steel and stainless steel products of a high quality. From the likes of windows, sliding gates and balustrades, etc. The company is owned by four members,namely;Markos Kgotleng,John Kamogelo Mohlala,Sicelo November and Frans Banele Hlabangani.We are .... young,ambitious and inspired by the disadvantaged backgrounds we come from.We also have different skillsets which when put together,we can make this business the greatest of its kind both in the country and around the world.This gives us an opportunity to uplift the community through providing employment and increase economic activity.
